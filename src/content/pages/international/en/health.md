Health onboarding should be treated as a priority continuity task during the first month in France.

## First 7 days checklist

- Identify university health support services.
- Prepare identity and status documents.
- Add health steps to the global admin tracker.
- Save emergency contact information.
- Organize all health-related proof files.
- Read official university onboarding notes.
- Plan weekly follow-up until status is clear.

## First 30 days checklist

- Start or finalize rights activation according to status.
- Verify receipt of official confirmations.
- Understand base coverage and optional complementary coverage.
- Archive all correspondence and proof documents.
- Update records after address/status changes.
- Plan a minimum monthly health budget.
- Clarify practical procedure for unexpected care.

## Vocabulary glossary (FR term -> EN explanation)

- **Assurance maladie**: public health coverage framework.
- **Carte Vitale**: health card used in routine care administration.
- **Mutuelle**: complementary health coverage.
- **Affiliation**: process of being registered in the system.
- **Remboursement**: reimbursement process.
- **Médecin traitant**: declared primary doctor.

## Practical example

Keep a dedicated health folder with confirmations, key dates and contacts. This reduces delays when additional proof is requested.

Rules vary by profile and can change. Always verify with official channels.
