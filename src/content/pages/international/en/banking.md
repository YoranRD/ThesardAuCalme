A stable banking setup simplifies housing payments, subscriptions and administrative continuity.

## First 7 days checklist

- Define immediate payment priorities (rent, transport, phone).
- Compare account options aligned with status constraints.
- Prepare required documents in one folder.
- Track submissions and pending requests.
- Build an initial monthly budget.
- Check transfer and account fees.
- Store account/security information safely.

## First 30 days checklist

- Stabilize one operational account.
- Configure critical recurring payments only.
- Verify payment and withdrawal limits.
- Build a minimum safety buffer.
- Archive banking proofs used in admin workflows.
- Avoid non-essential commitments in month one.
- Run a month-end budget review.

## Vocabulary glossary (FR term -> EN explanation)

- **RIB**: bank account identity details.
- **IBAN**: international account identifier.
- **Prélèvement**: direct debit.
- **Virement**: transfer.
- **Découvert**: overdraft.
- **Frais bancaires**: bank fees.

## Practical example

After activation, automate only essential payments and observe one full month before adding optional expenses.

For exact requirements, use official and institution-provided information.
