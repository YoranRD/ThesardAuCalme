This page provides general operational guidance for arrival. It is not legal advice. Official local instructions remain the source of truth.

## First 7 days checklist

- Secure temporary accommodation and keep proof.
- Activate a local phone number for administrative contact.
- Confirm key contacts at doctoral school and laboratory.
- Build one digital folder for all core documents.
- Create a deadline tracker for admin and research milestones.
- Identify campus health and international support contacts.
- Schedule an initial supervisor check-in.

## First 30 days checklist

- Start enrollment and archive every submission proof.
- Track residence-related steps according to personal status.
- Initiate health coverage onboarding.
- Set up a minimal research workflow (references, writing, backup).
- Activate university systems (email, library, portals).
- Protect a weekly routine with fixed deep-work sessions.
- Run a month-end review with supervisor/lab.

## Vocabulary glossary (FR term -> EN explanation)

- **École doctorale**: doctoral school supervising doctoral progression.
- **Laboratoire / labo**: research unit where supervision and projects are anchored.
- **Inscription administrative**: formal enrollment process.
- **Contrat doctoral**: funded doctoral employment contract.
- **Titre de séjour**: residence permit framework.
- **Assurance maladie**: French health coverage system.

## Practical sequence example

Week 1: institutional access + admin tracker.
Week 2: enrollment + residence file.
Week 3: health onboarding + weekly research system.
Week 4: document audit + supervisor progress checkpoint.

Always cross-check any critical step with official channels.
